From its small beginnings, VIVEDY cinemas have become one of the world's leading entertainment corporations. VIVEDY cinemas started in New Zealand has become available around the world and owns and operates over 1,000 cinemas with over 8,000 screens and more than 1,000,000 seats.

VIVEDY Careers

Here at VIVEDY, we know that our people are our greatest asset.
